About Pulsepoint Plugin

Pulsepoint is an open-source WordPress plugin that turns any site into a secure, all-in-one billing and fintech hub. Designed for developers and businesses, it enables fast, reliable, and seamless payments directly on your website.

With Pulsepoint Plugin, you can offer airtime top-ups, data bundles, electricity, cable TV, payouts, and virtual cards—all integrated into a single platform. Our mission is to simplify digital payments for WordPress sites worldwide.
